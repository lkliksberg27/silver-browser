How to use:

1. Click the Silver Browser icon in your toolbar (or press Alt+S) to start
2. Say "Alfred" (or "Alfredo" in Spanish) followed by a command
3. Alfred stays in conversation for 25 seconds — no need to repeat the wake word

Examples:
• "Alfred, read this page"
• "Alfred, open YouTube"
• "Alfred, click the login button"
• "Alfred, search for weather in Miami"
• "Alfred, translate this page"
• "Alfred, make the text bigger"
• "Alfredo, lee esta página"
• "Alfredo, traduce esta página"
• "Alfred, turn off"
